Her skjer syge ting

Heyy have you ever thought about how much stuff there is in the world? Like, straight-up crazy amounts of things. Let’s start with water. There’s 1.386 billion cubic kilometers of water on Earth!

Some random info

That’s enough to fill an infinite number of swimming pools. Then there’s air. Did you know you breathe in about 11,000 liters of air every day? Nuts, right? And what about trees? There are over three trillion trees on the planet. Insane, huh?

Just think about how much stuff every person owns. Clothes, shoes, phones, books… so many things, everywhere! And it just keeps growing and growing. It’s like the world is drowning in things, but we hardly notice because it’s all part of everyday life. Gyatt damn.
